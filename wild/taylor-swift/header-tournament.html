Structure

2 group stages followed by a 64-song bracket stage.

Group Stage 1

First, pick only one version of Teardrops on My Guitar to include (Radio mix).

Shuffle 175 songs into 35 groups of 5 songs each, top 3 from each group advance to next group stage.

Group Stage 2

105 songs remain, seed into 21 groups of 5 songs each, again top 3 from each advance to bracket stage.

To round it out to an even 64 songs, pick one eliminated song for a rebirth into the bracket stage.

Bracket Stage

64 songs go head-to-head to claim the title of Taylor's best song of all time!
